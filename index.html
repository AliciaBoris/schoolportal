About

Clairdot is a powerful, easy-to-use platform for schools to manage student records, track payments, and streamline administration all from a single dashboard. Designed for affordability and ease, it enables multiple schools to operate under one system while maintaining full data isolation.

How It Works

1. Schools register and receive a unique portal
2. Admins log in to add student and fee records
3. Teachers log in to update results, attendance
4. Management tracks data live across devices

Pricing

Clairdot is designed to be affordable for small and medium-sized schools.

  • Starter: ₦3,000/month (up to 50 students)
  • Standard: ₦5,000/month (up to 100 students)

  • Advanced: ₦10,000/month (up to 500 students)
