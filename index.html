About

Clairdot is a powerful, easy-to-use platform for schools to manage student records, track payments, and streamline administration all from a single dashboard. Designed for affordability and ease, it enables multiple schools to operate under one system while maintaining full data isolation.

How It Works

1. Schools register and receive a unique portal
2. Admins log in to add student and fee records
3. Teachers log in to update results, attendance
4. Management tracks data live across devices

Features

Clairdot School records management Portal boasts of unique and effective features that enable users to perform simple data management tasks seamlessly. These features include: Robust data management, login features for school administrators, student dashboards to enable students and parents view their information on the school portal, notification calenders to save information about school events which can be viewed on student dashboards, upload and download of students' results, unified fee tracking system to enable management keep track of fee payment and all these are at affordable costs.

School Administrator Login

A secure administrator login gives school officials exclusive access to management tools. Admins can monitor student records, manage staff, update results, and control system-wide settings, ensuring only authorized personnel can perform critical tasks.

Student Dashboard

Each student has a personalized dashboard where they can view their courses, assignments, attendance records, and academic progress. The dashboard provides quick access to essential information, making it easier for students to stay organized and focused on their learning journey.

The built-in school calendar allows administrators to broadcast important announcements, events, and deadlines. From examinations and PTA meetings to extracurricular activities, students and staff always stay informed with timely reminders and an up-to-date schedule.
